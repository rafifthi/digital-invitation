# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Wedding organizers and couples managing a digital invitation. They work primarily on desktop, moving between invitation design, guest management, sharing, gifts, and account settings. Their core need is to understand invitation readiness quickly and make changes without losing context.

## Product Purpose

Riuh Merekah is a focused workspace for publishing and operating a digital wedding invitation. Success means users can see what needs attention, edit confidently, manage guests efficiently, and share a finished invitation from one coherent dashboard.

## Brand Personality

Calm, precise, and lightweight. The interface should feel engineered and trustworthy while remaining warm enough for a personal milestone.

## Anti-references

Avoid ornate wedding stationery, romantic script typography, floral decoration, dense enterprise admin chrome, glassmorphism, oversized shadows, and colorful gradients used as generic decoration. The product should not resemble a template marketplace or a generic analytics dashboard.

## Design Principles

1. Make invitation readiness immediately understandable.
2. Keep standard dashboard interactions familiar and predictable.
3. Use color as functional punctuation, not decoration.
4. Preserve context while users move between editing and previewing.
5. Prefer clear hierarchy and generous rhythm over additional containers.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.1 AA. Maintain visible keyboard focus, semantic controls, sufficient contrast, reduced-motion support, readable text sizing, and interaction states that do not rely on color alone.
